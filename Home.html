With over 28 years experience in the IT services industry providing services to small, medium, large enterprises and government, Cosmic computers team has the expertise and the understanding of what is required to deliver quality and reliable services from support and services for structured networking products, security solutions such as CCTV, Access control, printing solutions, Servers and many more. We are the leading Integrated Information. Technology supplier of brands such as HP, Lenovo, Dell, Apple, Acer, Panasonic, Epson, CISCO,
TP-Link, Logitech and many others.

The Company currently has three branches in operation;

  • Kabelenga Branch; Situated along Kabelenga road is Cosmic Computers Head Office which provides total IT Support and Solutions in most parts of Zambia .

  • Mand Hill Branch; Located at shop number 16 Manda Hill Shopping Mall the branch offers a wide range of IT equipment and consumables.

  • Cairo Branch; Conveniently located in Zambias CBD our aim is to deliver a distinctive competitive advantage to our customers by combining technology and industry leadership with a complete focus on satisfying customer’s short and long term requirements.
